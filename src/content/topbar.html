Why do I see this?
Because you're awesome and you've installed Altruisto.com Chrome Extension. Anytime you visit a website of one of our partners you will see a bar at the top of a page giving you an opportunity to raise money for charities just by clicking one button. If you click on that button will be asked to confirm that you want to support altruisto through your purchases on the site you're viewing. After that, usually between 3-7% of your purchase will be donated to charities we support.

You can turn it off at any time by clicking at the altruisto.com Chrome Extension icon () in the top right of your browser and switching off the option "Inform me when I have an opportunity to raise money for charities from my purchase".
